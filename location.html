Weather

Summer in Santa Fe is beautiful. There are some things, however, you should know.

Santa Fe is considered the high desert, which basically means during the day it will be warm, 80-90s and no humidity. At night it will get cool, 60-70s, again no humidity, so you may want to have a light jacket handy.

July is also monsoon season, you can thank Adam for the date, so you should also be prepared for rain. That being said, if it does rain it's over in a flash.

Getting There
