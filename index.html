It only took 12 years

Excerpts from Adam's email/poem proposal to Danica. It tells the story of us.

A long time ago, it's been a while

Danica made Adam crack a smile

With what began what would be

11+ years of you and me

In that time

So much did pass

Stories to tell

Laughs to be laughed

The camp of the day was DFC

Unfortunately, camping outside of Shaver

was not meant to be

And though my dear Danica

This was the longest ago

It only took place in New Mexico

East we flew and back again

Rocky Roads here did begin

Life we explored

Each on a path of our own

Apart we did go

Together was unknown

But time, it teaches you things

and before too long we were back wing and wing

Now two is good

In fact, two is just fine

There are two of many things

Two is divine

But every now and again

There does come a time

Where two, though great

Is no longer just fine

So, (pet name for Danica), I've been thinking

And as much as thinking is fun

I must admit

I think it's time that two became one.
